## 🎨 CTA Design Guidelines

### **Text Overlay Specifications:**
- **Font**: Clear, readable sans-serif
- **Size**: Large enough for mobile viewing
- **Color**: High contrast against background
- **Position**: Top or bottom third of screen
- **Duration**: 2-3 seconds minimum

### **Button Design:**
- **Style**: App store button design
- **Color**: GettingStoned brand colors
- **Size**: Prominent but not overwhelming
- **Position**: Bottom center or corner
- **Animation**: Subtle pulse or glow effect

### **Logo Integration:**
- **Position**: Top corner or center
- **Size**: Prominent but not dominant
- **Consistency**: Same logo across all videos
- **Recognition**: Clear GettingStoned branding
