# 🪨 GettingStoned Manifesto Marketing Strategy

## 🎯 Core Messaging Framework

### **Primary Tagline:**
> "Not tracking your brain — rediscovering your mind"

### **Manifesto Pillars:**
1. **Beyond Neuroscience** - "Science maps neurons — karma shows causality"
2. **Transformation Not Calm** - "They sell calm — you offer transformation"
3. **Living Experiment** - "A living experiment in consciousness"
4. **Ethics Visible** - "They ignore ethics — you make it visible"
5. **Freedom Not Dependency** - "Building awareness that ends the need for apps"

## 🎬 Sora Video Strategy

### **Tier 1: Manifesto Hero Videos (30-60 seconds)**
These videos establish the philosophical foundation:

1. **`manifesto_hero`** - "The Modern Mirror of Mind"
   - Establishes the core concept of self-reflection
   - Shows transformation from digital overwhelm to contemplation
   - Features GettingStoned logo prominently

2. **`karma_vs_neuroscience`** - "Beyond Brain Scans"
   - Contrasts traditional brain monitoring with karma analysis
   - Emphasizes causality over correlation
   - Shows the choice between scientific measurement and spiritual understanding

3. **`transformation_not_calm`** - "Beyond Symptom Relief"
   - Contrasts GettingStoned with generic meditation apps
   - Shows transformation vs. temporary relief
   - Emphasizes deeper purpose

### **Tier 2: Philosophy Deep-Dive Videos (15-30 seconds)**
These videos explore specific manifesto concepts:

4. **`living_experiment`** - "Living Experiment in Consciousness"
   - Shows genuine engagement with ethical development
   - Emphasizes the app as a mirror, not just a tool
   - Features GettingStoned logo throughout

5. **`ancient_wisdom_modern_tech`** - "Ancient Wisdom Meets Modern Technology"
   - Bridges tradition and innovation
   - Shows continuity of spiritual practice
   - Emphasizes wisdom over technology

6. **`beyond_neuroscience`** - "Into Awakening"
   - Shows the choice between brain tracking and spiritual development
   - Emphasizes what science cannot measure
   - Features GettingStoned logo prominently

### **Tier 3: Differentiation Videos (10-20 seconds)**
These videos highlight competitive advantages:

7. **`ethical_visibility`** - "Making Ethics Visible"
   - Shows integration of mindfulness and morality
   - Contrasts with apps that ignore ethics
   - Features GettingStoned logo clearly

8. **`freedom_not_dependency`** - "Ending the Need for Apps"
   - Shows progression from dependency to independence
   - Emphasizes genuine spiritual development
   - Features GettingStoned logo throughout journey

9. **`modernization_not_monetization`** - "Modernization Not Monetization"
   - Contrasts with subscription-based apps
   - Emphasizes genuine spiritual development
   - Features GettingStoned logo as differentiator

10. **`who_you_really_are`** - "Action by Action"
    - Shows deep self-reflection and growth
    - Emphasizes the app as a mirror
    - Features GettingStoned logo prominently
