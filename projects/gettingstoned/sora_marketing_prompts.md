## Technical Specifications

### Video Settings for All Prompts:
- **Duration**: 15-60 seconds per video
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical) for mobile optimization
- **Quality**: High resolution for App Store and social media
- **Style**: Cinematic, modern, clean aesthetic
- **Lighting**: Natural, warm, spiritual feeling
- **Camera**: iPhone 15 Pro for authenticity
- **Color Palette**: Buddhist-inspired (gold, deep blue, white, earth tones)

### Content Guidelines:
- Show real people using the app authentically
- Emphasize the spiritual and technological aspects
- Include diverse users and settings
- Focus on transformation and growth
- Maintain respectful Buddhist imagery
- Highlight the AI and machine learning features

## Distribution Strategy:
1. **App Store**: Primary marketing video
2. **Social Media**: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
3. **Website**: Hero section and feature pages
4. **Investor Presentations**: Pitch deck integration
5. **Press Kit**: Media and journalist outreach
