# 🪨 GettingStoned: Ten Non-Virtues Cinematic Series
## Data-Driven Buddhist Parables Through Awareness

## 🎬 Series Overview

The **Ten Non-Virtues** series is a collection of 10 cinematic timelapse videos that transform Buddhist ethical teachings into data-driven visual narratives. Each video shows a person overcoming one of the ten non-virtuous habits through awareness, with real-time stone logging and karma visualization.

## 🎯 Core Concept

### **Narrative Arc (100 frames per video):**
1. **Ignorance (Frames 1-60)**: Person unconsciously performs non-virtue, logging ⚫ Black Stones
2. **Recognition (Frames 61-80)**: Small pauses appear, awareness begins to dawn
3. **Meditation/Turning Point (Frames 81-90)**: Person logs ⚫ Black Stone mindfully, then ⚪ White Stone
4. **Transformation (Frames 91-100)**: Harmful behavior stops, stones dissolve into light

### **Visual Elements:**
- **Split Screen**: Person's actions on left, karma graph on right
- **Stone Logging**: GettingStoned app UI with ⚪ White Stone and ⚫ Black Stone buttons
- **Data Visualization**: Gamma-shaped curve showing black stone accumulation and decline
- **Lighting Transition**: Grey → Amber → Golden → White
- **Sound Design**: Mechanical clicks → Soft chimes → Silence

## 🎬 Ten Non-Virtues Videos

### **1. Killing Harm**
- **Transformation**: From crushing insects/breaking objects → watering plants
- **Key Message**: "Karma exhausted — awareness remains"
- **Visual**: Early destruction, mid meditation, end plant care

### **2. Harsh Speech**

- **Key Message**: "Speech refined — peace revealed"

### **3. Gossip & Idle Talk**

- **Key Message**: "Truth spoken softly; awareness remains"

### **4. Stealing**
- **Transformation**: From taking without permission → returning items
- **Key Message**: "Generosity replaces grasping"

### **5. Sexual Misconduct**
- **Transformation**: From careless relationships → respectful connection
- **Key Message**: "Respect restores harmony — awareness remains"
- **Visual**: Symbolic relationship interactions, mid apology, end respect

### **6. Greed & Covetousness**
- **Transformation**: From obsessive checking → sharing/donating
- **Key Message**: "Contentment frees the mind"
- **Visual**: Checking possessions, mid sharing, end open window

### **7. Anger & Hatred**
- **Transformation**: From throwing objects → calm patience
- **Key Message**: "Patience cools the fire — awareness remains"

### **8. Wrong View**
- **Transformation**: From rigid opinions → balanced perspective

### **9. Deceit & Lying**
- **Transformation**: From small lies → honest truth

## 📊 Technical Specifications

### **Video Format:**
- **Duration**: 10-15 seconds per video
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical mobile)
- **Resolution**: 720x1280 minimum
- **Frame Rate**: 24fps cinematic
- **Style**: Cinematic timelapse with data visualization overlay

### **App UI Elements:**
- **GettingStoned Logo**: Prominently displayed on phone screen
- **Stone Buttons**: ⚪ White Stone and ⚫ Black Stone buttons
- **Stone Counter**: Visual jar filling with stones
- **Graph Animation**: Real-time karma curve updates
- **Metrics**: "Today 3⚫ 2⚪" counter

### **Visual Transitions:**
- **Color Flow**: Black → Grey → Amber → White
- **Lighting**: Mechanical → Warm → Golden → Pure
- **Sound**: Clicks → Chimes → Silence
- **Camera**: Static angle with slow dolly-in at turning point
